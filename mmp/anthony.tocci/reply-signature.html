Create a "Reply Signature"

A reply signature allows you to create a smaller, more concise signature block that will keep your identity present in the message thread, without being redundant or obnoxious by repeating the full signature sent in the initial message.

Signature settings in Gmail are located in Settings > General roughly two-thirds of the way down the page.
